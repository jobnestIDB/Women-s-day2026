Celebrating Women’s Strength & Spirit

Women’s Day is more than a celebration — it is a tribute to the strength, creativity, resilience, and leadership that women bring to every part of society.

Thathaastu India proudly brings together women from different walks of life to celebrate achievements, share experiences, and create opportunities for growth, creativity, and community connection.

Through activities like walkathons, creative showcases, skill experiences, and community events, this celebration aims to inspire confidence, encourage collaboration, and honour the incredible impact women make every day.

Beyond Women's Day

Women’s Day is not just a two-day celebration for us. It is the beginning of a long-term movement to build meaningful communities where women can connect, collaborate, and grow together.

Through this initiative, we will be creating interest-based communities for women across multiple domains including creativity, entrepreneurship, learning, social impact, and personal development.

Communities will be launching soon. Updates and joining details will be announced on this website and our Instagram page. Stay connected and be part of the journey.
